About me

Hello! I’m Skylar, a coder and project manager based in Eugene, Oregon. With over six years of experience in IT at a small company, I’ve recently expanded my skill set to include accounting and project management. My expertise spans languages such as Python, Ruby, JavaScript, and TypeScript, and I thrive on finding elegant solutions to complex problems.

As a queer mother, I bring a unique perspective to my work, blending creativity with analytical thinking. My academic background in Philosophy and Art History from the University of Oregon fuels my passion for uncovering the logic and beauty in how things are built. I’m excited to contribute to projects that reflect these values and help shape innovative solutions.

Feel free to connect with me to discuss potential collaborations or simply to share ideas!
